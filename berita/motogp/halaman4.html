Dovizioso: the attendance king

The italian, aling with teammate Jorge Lorenzo, has been competing on the World stage for 16 straight years -- the third highest on the grid behind Valentino Rossi (Moviestar Yamaha MotoGP) and Dani Pedrosa (Repsol Honda Team). It's a huge testament to 32-year-old, who in the last two years has been -- on paper - Marc Marquez' (Repsol Honda Team) most concistent challenger.
